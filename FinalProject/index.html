Dow and the Unemployment Rate

Using historical data of both the Dow Jones Industrial Average and the National Unemployment Rate, we are able to loosely analyze whether or not the Dow is affected by the Unemployment Rate, or vice versa.

Glossary of Terms
  • Dow Jones Industrial Average: a price-weighted measurement stock market index of the 30 prominent companies in the United States.
  • Unemployment Rate: officially a part of the Employment Situation, this defines the national average of those unemployed.
  • Average Directional Index: known as ADX, it is a technical analysis indicator used by traders to determine the strength of a trend; weak, flat, or strong.
